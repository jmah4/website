Guest Membership

Free

Our guest membership allows you to participate in short impromptu speech sessions called table topics. You may also take on any meeting roles except for speech evaluator.

Student Membership

Requires UofT Student Status

Our student membership allows UofT students to practice speeches for assignments or other important events. This membership tier includes guest privileges.

Toastmasters International Membership

Requires Paying Toastmasters International Fees @ 10USD/Month

By purchasing Toastmasters International membership you will have access to additional educational material and can deliver prepared speeches within the club. Expirenced speakers may also take on evaluator roles. This membership includes student and guest membership privileges.
